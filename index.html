The Water Hole Concept

Inspired by nature's gathering places that sustain life and build community.

Source of Life and Provision

Water holes universally represent life-giving sources. In ancient times, they provided essential water for survival, symbolizing provision and sustenance. In our context, they represent the flow of resources, information, and support between organizations.

Community and Connection

Throughout history, water holes served as communal gathering places where people met and bonded. They facilitated social interaction and connection. Our platform creates a digital "water hole" where community organizations can gather, connect, and collaborate.

Empowerment Through Access

Access to water has transformed lives by empowering communities. Similarly, access to shared resources, knowledge, and connections through our platform empowers organizations to increase their impact and better serve the community.

Our Mission

Water Hole: Connect brings together community organizations and nonprofits in the Greater Milwaukee area to break down silos, foster collaboration, and create a more efficient and effective support network for those in need.

By unifying our efforts, we can provide more comprehensive support, eliminate service gaps, reduce duplication, and maximize our collective impact on the community.
